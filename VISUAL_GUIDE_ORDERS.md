## Interactive Elements

### Hover Effects
- **Order Cards**: Elevate with shadow (translateY -5px)
- **Tab Buttons**: Color change on hover
- **Action Buttons**: Slight scale increase

### Click Actions

1. **"Browse Restaurants" Tab**
   - Switches to restaurants view
   - Shows restaurant carousel
   - Reinitializes Owl Carousel

2. **"My Orders (X)" Tab**
   - Switches to orders view
   - Loads user orders
   - Shows order cards

3. **Status Filter Dropdown**
   - Filters orders by selected status
   - Updates immediately on change
